# Privacy Policy for SmartHome

**Last updated: November 3, 2025**

## Introduction

SmartHome ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SmartHome mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Permission
- **Purpose**: Used for motion detection and video recording features
- **Data Storage**: All camera data is processed locally on your device
- **Data Sharing**: Camera data is NOT shared with third parties or uploaded to our servers

### Microphone Permission
- **Purpose**: Used for audio recording alongside video capture
- **Data Storage**: Audio is processed locally on your device
- **Data Sharing**: Audio data is NOT shared with third parties

### Internet Permission
- **Purpose**: Used to communicate with Telegram Bot API for remote control
- **Data Transmitted**: Only bot commands and status messages
- **Encryption**: All communications use HTTPS encryption

### Notification Permission
- **Purpose**: To show alerts and status updates from the bot service
- **Data Storage**: Notifications are handled by Android system

### Boot Completed Permission
- **Purpose**: To automatically start the bot service when device restarts
- **Impact**: No data collection associated with this permission

## Data Storage

All data collected by SmartHome is:
- Stored locally on your device
- NOT uploaded to any cloud servers
- NOT shared with third parties
- Deleted when you uninstall the app

## Third-Party Services

SmartHome uses the following third-party services:

### Telegram Bot API
- Used for remote control functionality
- You provide your own bot token
- Subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy: https://telegram.org/privacy

### Google Play Services
- Used for app updates
- Subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data (by uninstalling the app)
- Revoke permissions at any time through Android Settings

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
